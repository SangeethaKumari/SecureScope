# Access Control Policy - SecureScope Corp

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Last Revised:** January 16, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction
This policy defines the standards and procedures for managing access to SecureScope Corp's information systems. The goal is to ensure that only authorized individuals have access to the resources they need to perform their jobs.

## 2. Principle of Least Privilege
All users shall be granted the minimum level of access necessary to fulfill their professional responsibilities. Access rights must be reviewed quarterly by department heads.

## 3. User Identification and Authentication
### 3.1 Password Requirements
- Minimum length: 12 characters.
- Must include:Uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
- Rotation: Every 90 days.
- Account lockout: After 5 failed attempts.

### 3.2 Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
MFA is mandatory for all remote access and for accessing administrative interfaces or "Critical" systems (Tier 1).

## 4. Physical Access Control
### 4.1 Server Room Access
Access to the main server room is restricted to authorized IT infrastructure personnel only. All entries and exits are logged via biometric scanners. Visitors must be escorted at all times.

### 4.2 Clean Desk Policy
Employees must clear their desks of all sensitive information (printed or electronic) at the end of each workday.

## 5. Network Access Control
### 5.1 Remote Access (VPN)
Remote access to the internal network is permitted only via the company-approved VPN. Sessions will automatically terminate after 60 minutes of inactivity.

### 5.2 Firewall Changes
All requests for firewall rule modifications must be submitted via the Jira "Infra-Access" portal and require approval from the Chief Information Security Officer (CISO).

## 6. Onboarding and Offboarding
### 6.1 Onboarding
HR must notify IT of new hires at least 5 business days before the start date. Access is provisioned on the first day of employment.

### 6.2 Offboarding
Access for terminated or resigning employees must be revoked within 2 hours of their departure. Equipment must be returned to the IT desk within 24 hours.

## 7. Compliance and Auditing
Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination. SecureScope Corp reserves the right to audit access logs at any time without prior notice.
